Welcome!

To Yin-Yang Server

A friendly minecraft server for builders who also like survival vanilla minecraft.

Server Sections

PVP

Fight with other players looking for resources, while surviving the cruel minecraft world. Take what you want, anything goes.

PVE

Player damage is deactivated, you can come and relax with your friends while you survive the cold night and kill zombies and skeletons.

Hunger Games

A classic. A brutal battle to the death with limited time and space, and fighting against time and other people at the same time to avoid being killed. Will you be the last survivor?

Minigames

Minecraft is not only mining and crafting. Come and play with us on a game of paintball, bed wars, spliff and other action packed minigames.

Creative

We offer you a 100x100 area to build with whatever blocks you want. You don't like resource gathering? That's fine! Come build with us.
